PREVENTION


Strategies that limit the formation of urolithiasis (see pp. 1141, 1143, and 1138)


TECHNICIAN TIPS


Animals in acute renal failure should have urine output carefully quantified. One possible correctible cause of oliguria/anuria is
bilateral ureteral obstruction.


CLIENT EDUCATION


Strict adherence to dietary recommendations can minimize the risk of ureteral or urethral obstruction due to urolithiasis.
