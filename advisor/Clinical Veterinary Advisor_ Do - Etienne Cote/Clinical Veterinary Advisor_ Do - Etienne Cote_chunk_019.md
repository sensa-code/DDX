Remove lead objects and lead-based paint from pet's environment.


TECHNICIAN TIPS


If the patient is vomiting, note whether foreign material is in the vomitus (possibly eliminating the need for endoscopic/surgical
removal of lead foreign body from the GI tract). All solid lead must be removed from the GI tract prior to chelation therapy or else
chelation can extract lead from the foreign body, perpetuating toxicosis.


CLIENT EDUCATION


Keep pets out of areas where home renovation/remodeling is occurring.
