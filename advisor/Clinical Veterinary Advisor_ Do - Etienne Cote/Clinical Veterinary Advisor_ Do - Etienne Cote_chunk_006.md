CLIENT EDUCATION


Veterinarians are encouraged to keep a loaning library of books, newsletters, videos covering basic behaviors of dog and cats, and a
list of trainers and facilities that a staff member has verified use humane training techniques. This will provide the atmosphere of
caring that will make it easier for clients to seek and get the help they need. A useful source of video and other training and
behavioral material is http://abrionline.org/.


SUGGESTED READING


Donaldson J: The culture clash. Berkeley, Calif., 1996, James and Kenneth Publishers.


Miller P: The power of positive dog training. Dog Wise, 2003.


Seksel K: Training your cat. Melbourne, Australia, 2001, Hyland House.


AUTHOR: SORAYA V. JUARBE-DIAZ


EDITOR: KAREN L. OVERALL
